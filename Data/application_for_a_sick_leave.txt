Here is a sample application for a sick leave:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, Pincode]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Designation]
[Company/Organization Name]
[Company/Organization Address]
[City, State, Pincode]

Subject: Application for Sick Leave

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to inform you that, unfortunately, I am currently unwell and unable to attend work. Due to my health condition, I am requesting a sick leave from [start date] to [end date].

I am suffering from [mention the illness or condition, e.g., fever, cough, stomach problem, etc.]. My doctor has advised me to take complete rest and undergo treatment for the next [number] days. I have attached a medical certificate from my doctor, which confirms my illness and recommends the necessary leave.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am feeling better. If there are any urgent tasks that need my attention, please let me know so I can make arrangements to complete them remotely.

I would greatly appreciate it if you could approve my request for sick leave. If you require any additional information or documentation, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Thank you for your understanding and support during this time.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Note: Please attach a medical certificate from your doctor, if required by your organization. Also, make sure to keep a copy of the application for your records.
